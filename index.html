Chao Zhou

Assistant Professor · University of Lisbon

I am a phonologist working in the Department of General and Romance Linguistics at the University of Lisbon (ULisboa). My research lies at the intersection of language acquisition, phonology, and phonetics.

zhouchao at edu.ulisboa.pt

Research

A central thread in my research is to combine theoretical modelling (BiPhon-HG and OT) and experimental methods to understand how abstract phonological knowledge is acquired, represented, and shaped by multilingual experience.
