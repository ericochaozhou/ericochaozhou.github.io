Chao Zhou

Assistant Professor · Phonology and Language Acquisition

I am a phonologist working in the Department of General and Romance Linguistics at the University of Lisbon (ULisboa).

A central thread in my research is to combine theoretical modelling, including BiPhon-HG and OT, with experimental methods to understand how abstract phonological knowledge is acquired, represented, and shaped by multilingual experience.
