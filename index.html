Chao Zhou

Assistant Professor · Phonology and Language Acquisition

I am a phonologist working in the Department of General and Romance Linguistics at the University of Lisbon (ULisboa).

My work brings together formal phonological modelling and experimental methods to study how abstract phonological knowledge are acquired, represented, and shaped through multilingual experience.
