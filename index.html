Hi, I'm Chao Zhou.

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of General and Romance Linguistics at the University of Lisbon (ULisboa). Before joining ULisboa, I taught at the University of Minho (2021–2022) and the Polytechnic of Leiria (2021).

My research lies at the intersection of Second Language Acquisition and Phonology, focusing on how phonological systems interact with perception, production, and orthography in L2 speech.

  • Interaction between phonology and orthography in L2 speech
  • Structural effects on L2 speech perception and production
  • Acquisition of Mandarin tones and Portuguese stress
  • Development of rapid L2 proficiency tests (LextPT)

Research

My research explores phonological representation and learning in second language acquisition, with emphasis on orthography–phonology interaction, speech perception, and probabilistic modeling.

Key Topics

  • L2 phonological learning and variability
  • Orthography–phonology interaction
  • Speech perception and cue integration
  • Mandarin tone perception
  • Romance phonology and BiPhon frameworks

LextPT

LextPT is a rapid and validated vocabulary size test for L2 Portuguese proficiency. It provides a reliable 5-minute assessment of lexical knowledge.

  • Validated with 452 participants
  • European & Brazilian Portuguese
  • Robust across different L1 backgrounds
